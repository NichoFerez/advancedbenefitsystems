Identity Theft Protection Solution

Monitor for threats using Watson® AI

Our identity theft solution monitor for threats using IBM Watson Artificial Intelligence. This services monitors and processes billions of pieces of information to alert employees to potential threats that could compromise their identity.

Alert employees to use of their personal information Our solution alerts employees to the use of their personal information. Employees will be alerted to critical events—such as an account being opened in their name—so they can act if it wasn’t them. They will also be alerted you their information be found on the Dark Web.

Identity Restoration

In the event of identity theft, employees will be assigned a dedicated Customer Care agent to assist them every step of the way until their issues are resolved. Employees have insurance for up to $1 million for losses they experience because of identity theft, including stolen funds.
