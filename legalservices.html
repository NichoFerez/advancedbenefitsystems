Legal Services Solution

When employees face legal issues, getting help from high-quality attorneys can be expensive. Our legal services solution, enables you to offer legal resources for client’s employees – at no direct cost to you or them. Our legal services solution is an online resource designed to provide your employees with access to legal services. This powerful resource has prepared more than 5 million legal documents. This solution enables your clients to offer their employees assistance to….

  • Write a will
  • Get assistance with estate planning
  • Make power of an attorney
  • Prepare a healthcare directive

Your client’s employees start by choosing the legal service they need. By simply answering a series of questions, employees will have a document automatically tailored to their individual circumstances. During document preparation, employees can securely collaborate with another person, such as a relative or advisor. Once they’re done, they can export it for printing and signature.

Make navigating legal paperwork simpler by offering your clients this convenient and easy-to-use service. It’s a simple way to enhance your benefits package without affecting your budget.
